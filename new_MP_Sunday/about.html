ABOUT ME

Cora Geroux is a life coach, yoga teacher and writer. She is passionate about inspiring women to fall in love with their natural body, embrace the wholeness of who they really are, and live a kick-ass authentic life. Cora is an ERYT 200, RYT 500 international yoga teacher. She has been teaching transformative group yoga classes full time since 2009. Cora is also an ambassador for Lululemon and a faculty member for the Level 2 Teacher Training at Qi Health & Yoga in Manly.

Her passion lies at the intersection of western psychology, and eastern spirituality. At the age of 18 Cora began her study of Psychology at Simon Fraser University, and graduated in at 24 with a BA in Psych. Cora’s work and writing has been featured in numerous well known sources; such as Inspired Coach Magazine, Elephant Journal, The Lululemon Blog, The Wellness Project, The 21- Day Meditation Project, The Loving Earth Blog and countless other publications. Cora trained as a life coach with The Beautiful You Coaching Academy and credits this as one of the best decisions she has made to date.

MY STORY

+ 2004-09

I studied psychology and at Simon Fraser University and completed my BA in Vancouver, Canada.

I started to have severe anxiety attacks and wound up in counselling several times.

I became a yoga teacher & taught thousands of people how to create physical health. I still didn’t fit in. I wasn’t skinny or good at showy asana (postures).

I was cynical about the yoga world and lacked faith in the physical exercise we promoted as yoga.

I found my own way to manage my stress and anxiety through yoga & meditation

+ 2011

I fell in love with an amazing man, and moved to Sydney, Australia

I attended my first 10-day vispassana meditation retreat. Mind officially blown.

I stopped teaching yoga.

+ 2012-14

I got a full time job at a pub serving cheap steaks.

I committed to my meditation practice daily.

I met some amazing people that urged me back into teaching yoga. This time, I decided to take a risk and teach from my heart.
+ Even if no one came to my classes. I was terrified and excited.

I went back to vispassana in the Blue Mountains.

I made a promise to myself to trust my own inner wisdom above all else.

Yoga jobs started streaming in. I now had a platform to share my truth.

I became a published writer for the first time. Thanks Elephant Journal.

+ 2015

I created this website in the hopes of inspiring women all over the world to embrace who they really are.

I enrolled in the Beautiful You Coaching Academy to deepen my understanding of how to work one-on-one with women who want to fall in love with their natural body, define health and wellness on their own terms and lead a kick as authentic life.
